Intro

This website will help you with all of your schooling need. It is designed to give you tips and tricks alongside some good advice so you can pass your tests, get great marks on your CATs and improve your exam marks.

Tests

We all know how difficult it is to do tests, whether it comes to studying, practicing, time management or even controlling stress. It is not easy, but that is why this website is here. Here you will find some skills you need to know when preparing for a test, how to stress down before a test and tips for when you actually need to do your test.

CAT

In this section are some vital and key steps you should take when designing, planning and creating your CAT. CATs are not the easiest and that is why one of the main things you should do is work on your time management and your focus, there are many easy tips that you can find here to pick up your focus and improve your work ethic.

Assignments

Assignments are a lot like CATs except that they can be in many different forms; these are usually to do with the practical aspects of your subject. For example, if you got an assignment for science it would usually be an experiment like building a project or designing a building.
