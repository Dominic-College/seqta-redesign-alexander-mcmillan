Homework

Introduction

You can get homework for many subjects at a time and it can get stressful to deal with. This page will give you tips for managing your workload.

Organisation

The most important factor in dealing with homework is being organised. If you create a plan to complete your work, you will almost never get overwhelmed. Something that can be very helpful is using a wall or desk planner. You can write in the due dates of all of your homework and look at it whenever you are confused. When you complete a task, you can cross it off the planner and feel a sense of accomplishment.
